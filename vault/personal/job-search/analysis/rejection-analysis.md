## Category 1: Operating vs Building (22 jobs)

This is by far the dominant rejection theme. Simon consistently distinguishes between roles that maintain the status quo ("operating") and roles that create new capability ("building"). He wants to build.

### The Pattern

Simon uses the exact phrase "operating versus building" or close variants in his notes for nearly half of all rejections. The JDs that trigger this typically emphasise:
- Service level management and SLA adherence
- Incident, problem, and change management (ITIL)
- Day-to-day operational oversight
- Governance frameworks and compliance
- Vendor management and contract renewals
- Budget tracking and cost reporting
- Process documentation and procedures
